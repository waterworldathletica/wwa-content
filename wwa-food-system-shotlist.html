Editing Notes
Edit
Cold open clip reused at callback — same cut, no colour change
The cold open clip (Shot 01) is reused exactly at the callback moment. Same grade, same cut. No transition effect. It should feel like the same moment revisited, not a flashback. The viewer recognises it immediately.
Edit
Text overlays — statistics displayed as spoken, bold and high contrast
Every statistic gets its own text overlay appearing as Ash says it. 28.7%. 50%. 9%. 33%. More than triple. Bold, high contrast, large. The numbers are the argument — make them visible to muted viewers.
Edit
Hard cut to black — no fade, no music change, immediate
After "Right?" — hard cut to black immediately. Not a fade. The abruptness is intentional. Hold black for one full second. Caption CTA carries the conversion from there.
Edit
Grade — natural documentary feel, slightly cooler than gym content
This is not a gym shoot. Grade should feel real and slightly documentary — natural grocery store lighting, slightly cooled. Not the dark moody WWA gym palette. The rawness of the environment is part of the visual argument.
