[ Hard cut — silent from here. Music only. Text overlays carry everything. ]

— GOBLET SQUAT —
❌ Dumbbell away from body, rounded back → Henry holding DB away, back rounding
✅ DB braced to chest, heels elevated, neutral spine → Ash sets position on ramp
❌ Spine winks, no knee tracking, fast reps → Uncontrolled, no depth, no thought
✅ Knee over toe, neutral spine throughout, controlled → Ash cues knee path and tempo

— ROMANIAN DEADLIFT —
❌ Squatting the weight, hips rising and falling → Henry treating it like a squat
✅ True hip hinge — hips back and forward → Ash cues the hinge pattern
❌ Knees over toes, back rounding, shins not vertical → Lower back at risk
✅ Fixed knee bend, vertical shins, neutral spine, slow exhale at top → Heels driving

— LEG EXTENSION —
❌ Flinging weight, machine losing contact → Henry kicking up fast, pad lifting off legs
✅ Pad under knees, controlled hold at top → Ash places pad, cues upper body brace
❌ No control, tension dropping constantly → Fast descent, no eccentric
✅ Slow eccentric, exhale at top, core engaged → Every rep deliberate

— CALF RAISE —
❌ Seated machine, fast pulse, no stretch → Henry pulsing with no heel drop
✅ Smith machine + platform, full heel drop → Ash sets up the platform under bar
❌ No range, heels never touch ground → Just noise
✅ Full stretch, slow eccentric, exhale and flex at top → Only the calves move

[ Closing shots — no text ]
Wide — Ash, abs to lower body in frame
Punch in — quad flex, muscle separation visible. Hold.

The one thing that makes or breaks this shoot: the spine wink on the goblet squat and the squatting pattern on the RDL need to be visibly obvious on camera. If the mistakes look subtle, the corrections look unnecessary. Exaggerate the bad. The lower back risk has to read clearly — that's what makes this video feel urgent rather than educational.

Editing Notes
Edit
Hook to corrections — hard cut, no transition
Hook line ends. Hard cut straight into the first correction. No fade. The abruptness signals the video is shifting gears.
Edit
Text overlays — ❌ red, ✅ green. One at a time.
Every mistake and correction labelled on screen. Red X for the mistake, green tick for the correction. Text appears as the action happens. Never two labels simultaneously.
Edit
Closing shots — no text, no cut
Wide lower body shot holds 2–3 seconds. Punch in to the quad flex. No text on either closing shot. Let the physique land in silence.
