LEFT: Most People — RIGHT: WWA

Most People — Goblet Squat: Dumbbell held away from body. Rounded back. Spine winking at the bottom. Fast uncontrolled reps. No knee tracking. No braced core. Poor range.
WWA — Goblet Squat: Heels elevated on ramp. Dumbbell firmly braced to chest. Neutral spine, shoulders retracted, chest out. Braced core. Slow descent with knee tracking over toe. Never winks. Controlled drive to the top.

Most People — Romanian Deadlift: Squatting the weight. Hips rising and falling. Knees coming over toes. Shins not vertical. Lower back rounding. No hinge pattern.
WWA — Romanian Deadlift: Weight tracks vertical shins. True hip hinge — hips move back and forward, not up and down. Knee bend fixed throughout. Neutral spine never winks. Slow controlled pace. Exhale at the top. Heels driving the movement targeting hamstrings.

Most People — Leg Extension: Flinging the weight. Machine losing contact with legs at the top. Fast, uncontrolled. No upper body brace. Tension constantly changing.
WWA — Leg Extension: Pad or sponge under knees for maximum knee flexion. Controlled flex and hold at the top. Upper body fully braced, neutral spine, core engaged. Exhale at the top. Slow eccentric return. Every rep deliberate.

Most People — Calf Raise (Seated Machine): Too much weight. Fast pulsing — tippy toe stand. No heel lowering. No full stretch. No range. Just noise.
WWA — Calf Raise (Smith Machine + Platform): Platform under the bar allows full heel drop and stretch. Lengthened position maximises tension on the calf. Slow controlled movement. Priority on the eccentric. Heels tap the ground. Drive up, exhale, flex. Only the calves move. Everything else locked.
