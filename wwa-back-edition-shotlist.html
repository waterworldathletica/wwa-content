Most People — Pull Up: Knees swinging for momentum. Shoulders doing the work. Fast, sloppy, partial reps. Arms never fully extend. Never sinks into the back.
WWA — Pull Up: Arms fully extended overhead. Slow controlled pull. Internal elbow rotation. Core engaged, heels elevated, pelvis forward, slight lean back from the bar. Two pull ups to their five or six. Two angles — side and back.

Most People — Lat Pulldown: Too upright. Pulling into the shoulders. Wrists bending at the bottom. Body weight momentum flinging the bar. Fast. No range. No contraction.
WWA — Lat Pulldown: Proper hand width. Internally rotated elbows exposing the lats. Full range — arms extended overhead to elbows tucking into side pockets. Bar to clavicle. Slow negative. Braced core. Side profile shot both sides.

Most People — Seated Cable Row: Small A-frame attachment. Body jerking back and forth. Arms and legs doing the work. Fast. No stretch. No contraction. No pause.
WWA — Seated Cable Row: Wide attachment. Full protraction at the start — arms lengthened, shoulder blades spread, core braced toward the load. Low shoulders, low elbows pulling into chest. Full retraction at the top. Pause. Elbows scoop down toward side pockets. Slow controlled return to full stretch.

Most People — Face Pull: Rope attachment, poor stance, no core. Leaning back with bodyweight to pull. Fast. Messy. No range.
WWA — Face Pull: Braced core, proper foot stance. Full arm extension with protracted shoulder blades into the stretch. Elbows wide and high, rope overhead at the top. Flex and hold 1–1.5 seconds. Slow controlled return to full stretch.
