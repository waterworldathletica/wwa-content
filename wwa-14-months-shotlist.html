Editing Notes
Edit
Photo overlays — before and after timed to delivery
Before photo drops on "assuming that just showing up will give you something to show off." After photo drops on "14 months later." Each holds for 2–3 seconds minimum. Don't rush the reveal — that's the scroll-stop moment.
Edit
Text overlay — every line on screen as it's spoken
One line at a time. Clean minimal font, white or off-white. Never two lines simultaneously. Muted viewers need to follow the full script through text alone.
Edit
Grade — WWA palette, cool and sharp
Gym lighting runs warm. Correct it. Crushed blacks, cooler midtones. Consistent across talking head and both cutaways.
